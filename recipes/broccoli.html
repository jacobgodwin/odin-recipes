Roasted Broccoli

Description

Crispy, roasted broccoli with a hint of lemon.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Heads of Broccoli
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F
  2. Wash and chop up your broccoli into florets.
  3. Toss the broccoli in about 3 - 4 TBSP of olive oil, season with a generous pinch of salt and few cranks of black pepper, and spread it out on an aluminum foil lined baking sheet. (The aluminum foil makes for a quick cleanup)
  4. Roast the broccoli for about 15 minutes, then give it a toss and roast for about 15 more minutes or until the broccoli is crispy and has a slight char to some of the florets.
  5. Remove from the oven and immediately squeeze the juice of half of a lemon and serve.
